Introduction

July 1, 2001, we started our journey with the name and style of “BENGAL REMEDIES”, a Marketing and Trading House of pharmaceutical products, on 1st July 2001 the particular day that is regarded as Doctors’ day, the birthday and the day of peaceful demise of Dr. Bidhan Chandra Roy. (Born in 1882, 1st July in Patna and demise on 1st July 1962). The journey of thousand and thousand miles begins with one step which never stops. It is a great pleasure on our part that we celebrate our each and every foundation day in several parts of india and also in Kolkata (specially in ACADEMY OF FINE ARTS, 2, Cathedral Road, Kolkata – 700071) We are just not a business organization – we are a family, a Pariwar, we don’t work just for the sake of a Company. We work for ourselves for the growth of our Pariwar. “Emotion gives the motion of a life” and emotional involvement enhances the degree of productivity, and it is needless to say that a man can never achieve to the tune of 100% without the combination of emotion crowned with Professionalism. We are neither the employees nor the employer or distributor; we are all the member of an “Emotionally integrated family”

Our Goal

We aim at serving the nation, particularly the rural part of the country and not just the urban crowd, backed by our capital of honesty, hard work and sincerity and generate further employment opportunities which is crucial now-a-days throughout the country. .

Company history

The toddler of 2001 has put its firm step in this year with a bigger endeavor that has a crave to make Bengal Remedies not only a sales promotion organization in the era of medicinal business but also to make it a cradle of Family for medical professionals. To help to build a medical sales career is going to be like dream comes true experience for Bengal Remedies, whose objective is to make a promising career which will help the aspirants to learn how to become successful with sheer dint of hard work and business acumen. The combined course of technical and business knowledge touched with business communication and human relationship is going to give the aspirants a strong foothold in their field of success. Bengal Remedies has decided to provide its service in all time throughout the year, so that any type of aspirants (working or non-working) can opt for the trade. Being a successful medical business sales promotion organization for more than a decade and having more than 1000 products in the market we can ensure of giving a successful career in the domain of medical professional. Bengal Remedies is in search of those aspirants who are dreaming with their career in the medical sales field. So, now stop dreaming and come forward to make it true..
